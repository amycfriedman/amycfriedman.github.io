Would you like to move away from bad habits, unhelpful thoughts, painful emotions, damaging relationships ?

Would you like to learn new ways to interact, to respond, to be happy ?

Together we will understand the how and the why you do and feel as you do, and move toward a more contented and confident you.

I look forward to working with you starting from where you are and setting the compass for where you’d like to go. Each of my clients will find that I am genuinely interested, patient, kind and determined to tailor our work to who you are, your strengths, your lifestyle, and needs.

No old school “uh huh” in my sunlit office. Clients find me engaging, energetic, empathic, creative and funny when appropriate. As a lifelong learner, mother, ex-wife, I work from the latest research, my own life experience to understand difficulties born of family dysfunction, trauma, temperament and current circumstance.

I invite you to please read through my website and call when you are ready for the next step: – a brief phone chat to go over your particular situation; and then to schedule our first appointment.
